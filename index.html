Oxford Physics Profile

I'm a senior research computing associate at the University of Oxford, where I do research and teach in the sub-department of Atmospheric, Oceanic & Planetary Physics.

Previously I was a senior oceanographer at Fugro GEOS Ltd where I was responsible for the development and deployment of numerical models for operational weather forecasting and the production of hindcast models for the company's consultancy group.
